Engine Showcase

Feature 1

Vortex FX offers a comprehensive suite of tools to facilitate precise object selection and manipulation within the grid, utilizing intuitive gizmos. Additionally, our physics simulation capabilities enable you to apply trajectory forces, allowing for realistic and dynamic interactions

Feature 2

VortexFX enables seamless integration with popular 3D modeling software, allowing you to import FBX assets from tools like Blender and others. These assets can then be used to simulate realistic physics interactions, including complex bone rigs, with precise control and accuracy.
